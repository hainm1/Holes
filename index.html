Camp Green Lake

"If you take a bad boy and make him dig a hole every day in the hot sun, it will turn him into a good boy."
SURVIVAL GUIDE:

• DIGGING: Walk out into the desert. Hold SPACE to dig until your hole is 5 feet deep.
• HYDRATION: Digging and walking drain water. Drink from the Water Truck in camp (Press E).
• HAZARDS: Sandstorms slow you down. Yellow-Spotted Lizards will bite you causing you to pass out.
• UPGRADES: Press P to open the Trading Post and spend completed holes on better gear.
