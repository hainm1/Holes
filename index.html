Survival Guide

GOAL: Survive the harsh desert of Camp Green Lake by digging holes.

  • Water: Your water drains constantly. If it hits zero, you collapse!
  • Energy: Digging and jumping use energy. Stand still to regenerate it.
  • Digging: Hold SPACE to dig. Requires energy.
  • Shop: Press 'P' to open the store. Spend your dug holes to buy upgrades (max 3 times each).
  • Dangers: Watch out for Yellow-Spotted Lizards! (They roam the desert).
